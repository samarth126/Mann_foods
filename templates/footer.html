About Us

MAAN FOODS, founded by Mr. Abhijeet Singh Vaish in 2019, is an ISO 9001&22000 certified company based in Indore. They specialize in manufacturing and trading various food products, including Indori Poha, Indori Namkeen, Sabudana, and Dalia. They offer pre-mix options like Khaman, Idli, Moong Bhajiya, Dahi Vada, Bedmi Poori, and Paneer Gravy. Ready-to-eat dishes such as Poha and Upma, as well as dessert pre-mix options like Moong Halwa and Gulab Jamun, are also available. With their ISO certifications, MAAN FOODS ensures quality and food safety, catering to the diverse tastes of customers
